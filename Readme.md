# 📌 Project Overview

The **Online Food Delivery Analysis** project provides a comprehensive business intelligence solution for analyzing food delivery operations using **SQL, Python, PostgreSQL, and Power BI**.

The dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor customer behavior, restaurant performance, delivery efficiency, revenue trends, and operational KPIs to support data-driven decision making.

---

# 🎯 Business Objectives

- Analyze customer ordering behavior
- Measure restaurant performance
- Monitor delivery efficiency
- Identify delayed deliveries
- Understand revenue trends
- Evaluate payment preferences
- Improve operational efficiency

# 💡 Business Insights

- Peak hours contribute significantly to total order volume.
- Longer delivery distances are associated with higher delivery times.
- Certain restaurants consistently generate higher revenue and ratings.
- Digital payment methods dominate customer transactions.
- Delivery delays are concentrated in specific cities and time windows.
- Customer satisfaction improves with shorter delivery times.
