Overall Dataset Insight
•	Customer demand is evenly distributed across cities, areas, genders, and cuisine types, indicating a balanced and unbiased dataset. 
•	Around 85% of orders are completed successfully, reflecting good operational reliability. 
•	Delivery delays remain the primary operational concern, with 63% of deliveries classified as delayed. 
•	Most customers place medium-value orders, receive moderate discounts, and order from restaurants located within 10 km. 
•	The business has opportunities to improve customer satisfaction by reducing long delivery times and minimizing delay-related cancellations.
